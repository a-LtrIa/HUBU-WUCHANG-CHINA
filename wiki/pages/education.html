Science popularization month of synthetic biology

The Background

In the course of the survey, we found that at present, the Chinese people still have great misunderstandings about synthetic biology technology, and science popularization activities need to be more interesting and diversified. In order to popularize the knowledge of synthetic biology, improve the public's interest and cognition of life science, and promote the wide application of synthetic biology technology in society, our team planned and organized the general month of synthetic biology.

Parties and media

In order to solve the problem of activity funds and promotion degree, we sought the support of funds or activity platforms from the Chinese society of cell biology, Hubei society of cell biology, Hubei provincial science popularization education base, College of life sciences, Hubei University, the State Key Laboratory of biocatalysis and Enzyme Engineering jointly built by the Province and the Ministry, the national experimental teaching demonstration center of chemical and biological engineering technology of Hubei University, and the Committee of the Art College of Hubei University of the Communist Youth League.
In the middle of the event, we published the personal blog of the event on Changjiang online ,which was widely praised by netizens. The editorial department of Changjiang Daily immediately began to close our event and carried out media promotion for us.
After the event, the official wechat official account "cell world" of the Chinese Society for cell biology also released the event tweet, expressing its affirmation of the effectiveness of our event.

Event preparation

We invited the iGEM teams hubuloong CHN, China Hubu Wuhan, tjusls China and CUG China to jointly organize the activities. They brought their laboratory research and iGEM projects to the activities, which broadened the science popularization field of the activities.

Purpose

1. We hope to guide college students majoring in art to interpret their attitude towards synthetic biology changing the world from their professional teaching and reading. We have held the competition as a "campus version of iGEM for cross-border art". We hope that the contestants can think with us about the current or potential survival problems faced by human beings in the world and how we can make our contributions from the perspective of synthetic biology.
2. We set up the track according to iGEM village, and asked the contestants to reflect their solutions or reflections in their works. We know that for them, it is likely to be more difficult to give a scientific and feasible solution, but this is what we hope they can be guided to do. In order to encourage them, we have been in close contact with them. We answer any questions on the basic knowledge of synthetic biology for them, and we have set the evaluation standard that gives solutions will score higher than reflection (jump to the next part of the scoring standard). We hope to find art majors who are interested in biological art in the competition. If they can further study in the field of biological art and further exchange and study with synthetic biology in the future, we believe that our competition is a real success.
3. Participants and teamwork

This event attracted primary and secondary school students, parents, college students, graduate students, educators, science and technology lovers, etc. At the same time, we have also received the support and help of many cooperation units, including schools, social academic groups, media, etc. In the process of the event, the volunteer team played an important role. The team members worked closely together to complete the task of the event and played a good role in educating all kinds of audiences of the event.

Speech Contest

In China, speech competitions with party and government themes are popular ideological education activities among Chinese teachers and students on university campuses, regardless of the major or college. We have found that we can take advantage of this opportunity to introduce our project and plant synthetic biology to non biological teachers and students. In terms of speech format, we will take China's food security as the starting point. In the speech, we talked to the audience about the development of rice breeding technology and the research process of polyploid rice, and introduced our project to the audience. At the end of our speech, we call on more college students and young people to actively engage in the fields of synthetic biology and agriculture, in order to promote the development of agriculture and society.

Volunteer Teaching

What is biology studied for? What is synthetic biology?
Our team found that the public was not familiar with the new concept of synthetic biology, and had not even heard of it. This made our iGEM team feel ashamed, so we came up with the idea of popularizing synthetic biology knowledge to the public.
In order to showcase and introduce biology, more precisely synthetic biology, to the public, our team conducted a 6-day summer teaching activity in Xinhu Village, Datan Office, Changjiang New District, Wuhan City, Hubei Province from July 20 to July 25, 2024, hoping to enhance everyone's understanding and interest in the new field of synthetic biology.

How to make it easy for children to understand synthetic biology does not seem to be a difficult problem for students at Hubei University. With the collaboration of students majoring in biotechnology and art, a brilliant fusion of biology and art has been initiated, and the concept of synthetic biology is fully demonstrated at this moment.

We brought tools for making glass slides and microscopes for observing glass slides. At the same time, we collected lotus leaves and rice leaves locally and took children to use dissection tools to make temporary glass slides. We carefully ensure that children use the dissecting knife safely and use tweezers to place the cut material on the cover glass. The children actively participated in this course, demonstrating admirable learning, observation, and hands-on abilities. "We didn't know before that life is so magical. Under the microscope, I saw such a wonderful world of cells, and through this course, my hands-on abilities were greatly exercised

Lectures

We found through survey questionnaires and public opinion analysis that the panic of the Chinese people towards rice related technologies has been excessively intensified. They reject almost all technologies except for hybrid breeding technology, and even have a passionate attitude towards practitioners. This is mainly due to the lack of life science knowledge and erroneous popularization among ordinary people. In order to improve the public's unscientific cognition and alleviate their panic, we held special lectures in communities, high schools, university campuses, and accounting offices, explaining the development of rice breeding technology and the current application of synthetic biology technology in rice breeding to community residents, high school students, environmental engineering majors, and accountants. Through illustrations and storytelling, we explained in detail the process of human nutrient intake to metabolism, hoping to help them understand and distinguish the differences between modern breeding technologies. We also explained to them the fundamental reasons for the safety controversy surrounding genetically modified technology and the reasons why national laws prohibit its promotion, hoping to help them identify the essential differences between genetically modified technology and other modern breeding technologies, and change their current unscientific perception of "non hybridization is genetically modified".

At the same time, we noticed that our target audience included children who had not yet learned about biology. Therefore, we conducted simple experiments during the lecture to stimulate their curiosity about biology by reducing the color reaction between sugar and Fehling reagent, protein and biuret reagent.
